About Us

Anandotsav Exhibition is a premier exhibitions & events company dedicated to creating immersive experiences. We arrange exhibitions in various cities, bringing together businesses, artists, and the public under one roof.

Founded with the vision to celebrate vibrancy and enterprise, our exhibitions showcase the best in lifestyle, handicrafts, innovation, and regional specialties. With a dedicated team and years of event expertise, we ensure every event reflects excellence, engagement, and seamless execution.

From Sawantwadi to metropolitan hubs, our footprint continues to grow, creating valuable networking opportunities and unforgettable moments for exhibitors and visitors alike.
